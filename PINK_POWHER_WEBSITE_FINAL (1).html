Who We Are

More than a nonprofit — a movement

We are a group of passionate young women who understand that awareness without action means nothing. Pink Powher bridges education, community support, and tangible change — raising funds and voices for breast cancer patients across our communities.

To fund treatment support and recovery services for those living with breast cancer, especially those with limited access to care.

🔍

Awareness First

Our Commitment

Every dollar goes toward people, not overhead

Pink Powher operates with complete financial transparency. As a student-run nonprofit, we keep administrative costs at zero — every dollar raised goes directly to patient support, awareness programs, and community outreach.

We are committed to centering the voices of survivors, advocates, and medical professionals in everything we do.

99% vs 28%

The 5-year survival rate for localized (early-stage) breast cancer is 99%. For distant (late-stage) cancer, that drops to just 28%. Early screening is not just important — it is life-saving.

Source: American Cancer Society — Cancer Facts & Figures 2023; Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results (SEER) Program, National Cancer Institute.

Early Detection Awareness

Through community events, educational campaigns, and partnerships with healthcare providers, we encourage everyone to prioritize regular screenings — because when we catch it early, we win. Early detection is the single most powerful tool in the fight against breast cancer.
